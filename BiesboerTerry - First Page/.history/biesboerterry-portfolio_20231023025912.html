What can i say about thatdudeterry that hasn't been said? I am a proud father, avid napper, terrible gamer, and self proclaimed nerd. I currently reside in St. Louis, MO with my daughter Joley, and our pug Yoshi. I enjoy drawing and painting as well as sleeping. All the sleep.

I am currently enrolled at Full Sail University for a Bachelors Degree in Web Development and Design. In my (limited) free time I enjoy playing racquetball with Joley, walking and catching Pokemon with Yoshi, and making art or playing video games. And sleep, can't forget sleep.

I have always had a love for technology, and after we got thw family computer in 1995 I was hooked. This led me to learning html when I was first introduced in 1997. These days I am enjoying learning these new languages and I'm looking forward to learning even more! Did I mention sleep?
