Street Art Photography

Street Art photography combines two of my favorite mediums in one fantastic display. I have been fascinated by graffiti since I was a freshman in high school. Over the years I went from very amateur drawings to more involved pieces. Eventually I went on to using real spraypaint (don't tell my mom), and, while I was good with a pen and a pad, I wasnt quite up the ranks when it came to a spraycan. That is where these two loves combine, Something I can appreciate and marvel at simultaneously.Of course, art is subjective, and many may not appreciate the beauty of graffiti. While I may not be here to try and change anyones mind, I will share with you some examples from artists that I admire.
